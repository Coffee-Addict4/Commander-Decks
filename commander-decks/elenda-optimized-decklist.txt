## KEY STRATEGY NOTES:
1. **Early Game**: Deploy cheap creatures, draw engines, and mana rocks
2. **Mid Game**: Start sacrifice chains to grow Elenda and trigger drains  
3. **Late Game**: Sacrifice Elenda for tokens, deploy anthems to win

## INFINITE COMBOS:
- Karmic Guide + Reveillark + sac outlet = infinite ETB/death triggers
- Any creature + Nim Deathmantle + Ashnod's Altar = infinite tokens/mana

## MANA CURVE OPTIMIZATION:
- 1 CMC: 8 cards (Viscera Seer, Sol Ring, Swords, Path, etc.)
- 2 CMC: 12 cards (Signets, draw spells, small creatures)
- 3 CMC: 15 cards (Removal, medium threats)
- 4+ CMC: 10 cards (Win conditions, high-impact)
- Lands: 36 cards (Optimal for consistency)

This build prioritizes efficiency, consistency, and multiple win conditions while maintaining the core aristocrats strategy.
